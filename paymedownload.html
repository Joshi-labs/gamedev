How to install

1.Download latest version of PayMe.

2.Go to your file browser and click on install (one popup window will appear you have to tap on "install anyway")

How to use

1. sign up then login

2. watch ads (Total of 100 ads) and press request button.

3. After 24 hours click on history button and you will see a 10 rupees google play giftcard.

4. go to playstore and click on your profile

5. click on Payments and subscriptions

6. click on redeem a giftcard and type that giftcard you recieved.

7. and then go spend that money on your favourite game.
